From deep within the Cybersphere, a flash of psychedelic colour and a deep laugh emiited, followed by a great rush of smoke and suddenly, and there on the couch sits a tall hooded figure, the sound of gamma goblins subsides and the Freaky Bob asks "Where's the party?".

Inspired from the start by Perry and Bradley ripping up the cheesy chip refectory at DeMontfort Uni he took a trip down to London and bought some CDJ's and a mixer with a briefcase of fivers, and was immediately laughed at, but now everyone uses CD's innit, so he's moved to digital.

Andy has been banging out annoying Techno usually off his chops for years.
